Autobots

The Autobots are the good guys from Cybertron. They are the main protagonists of the Transformers universe and are symbolized by their colorful array of primary colors.

Optimus Prime

Optimus Prime, originally known as Orion Pax, became the next Prime by receiving the Matrix of Leadership, as he was chosen by the High Council. As the new prime, Megatron betrayed Optimus as he felt the title of Prime was stolen from him.

Decepticons

The Deceptions are the bad guys from Cybertron. They are the main antagonists of the Transformerse universe and are symbolized by their colorful array of purple-like and red colors.

Megatron

Megatron was originally called Megatronus, and wanted the betterment of his world Cybertron. His desire for a reformed world turned into tyranny, leading him to fall under a dark path as a Deception.
