Autobots

The Autobots are the good guys from Cybertron. They are the main protagonists

of the Transformers universe and are symbolized by their colorful array of primary colors.

On the defensive side, the Autobots faced against the tyrannical attacks of the Decepticons, fighting for the values of

peace and justice. With the dying Cybertron, they sought energy, through the Cybertronian life source of energon. They landed

on Earth, and with the Decepticons doing so as well, they fight to protect the fate of humanity.

Optimus Prime

Optimus Prime, originally known as Orion Pax, became the next Prime by receiving the Matrix of Leadership, as he was chosen by the High Council.

As the new prime, Megatron betrayed Optimus as he felt the title of Prime was stolen from him.

In the Michael Bay Transformers universe, Optimus Prime protects the All Spark from the oncoming onslaught of Decepticons. He defeats and kills Megatron

to ensure the safety of humanity. Upon Megatron's revival and partnership with the Fallen, Optimus is faced with the task of defeating them, in which he

destroys the Fallen, but lets Megatron flee. Finally, Optimus ends Cybertronian threats to Earth, killing both Sentinel Prime and Megatron for good.

Decepticons

The Deceptions are the bad guys from Cybertron. They are the main antagonists

of the Transformers universe and are symbolized by their colorful array of purple-like and red colors.

Their goal as a military faction is to seize control over all of Cybertron and its resources, specifically energon. Energon is the

source of all Cybertronian life. After arriving onto Earth, the Decepticons' mission became the conquering and control over the planet

and it's human life forms.

Megatron

Megatron was originally called Megatronus, and wanted the betterment of his world Cybertron.

His desire for a reformed world turned into tyranny, leading him to fall under a dark path as a Deception.

In the Michael Bay Transformers universe, he desires the All Spark, which can turn any object into a living

Transformer. Ironically he is killed by it. Even more ironically, his subordinate Decepticons take a shard

of the All Spark in order to revive him to fight Optimus once again. Optimus defeats Megatron and his new partner

the Fallen, leaving Megatron disfigured. Megatro goes into hiding and takes on a disfigured and rusted look. Only then,

is the trilogy confirmed with the final death of Megatron by Optimus' blade.
